## üí∞ **EQUITY SPLIT RECOMMENDATIONS:**

### **Standard Split: 50/50** ‚≠ê Recommended

**You get:** 50% (for building the entire technical foundation)
**Co-founder gets:** 50% (for building the business)

**Why this works:**
- Fair to both sides
- You built the MVP, they'll scale it
- Both are equally important

### **Alternative: 60/40**

**You get:** 60% (if you've done significantly more work)
**Co-founder gets:** 40% (if they're joining later)

**Use this if:**
- MVP is very complete
- You have some early traction
- Co-founder is less experienced

### **Vesting Schedule:** (CRITICAL!)

**Both co-founders should have 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff:**
- Year 1: 25% vests
- Years 2-4: Monthly vesting (2.08% per month)

**This protects both of you if someone leaves early**

   - Demands 60%+ for joining
   - Doesn't value what you've built
   - Red flag for future conflicts

5. **Won't sign agreements**
   - No co-founder agreement
   - No vesting schedule
   - Verbal only

   - Knows potential customers

4. **Cultural fit**
   - Similar work ethic

   - Can work together under pressure
