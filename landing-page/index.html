What MeatHub is

MeatHub is a hyperlocal marketplace that connects customers with verified local butchers. We enable direct ordering, transparent sourcing, and flexible delivery options.

The platform supports multiple use cases: regular household orders, gym protein packages, pet meat subscriptions, and B2B partnerships. Customers can choose their preferred butcher, view preparation through video transparency, and manage recurring deliveries.

Built on a modern microservice architecture, the system uses AI to enhance user experience and optimize inventory management, with blockchain integration for supply chain traceability.

Why it's different

  • Choose your butcher — transparency in who prepares your order
  • Transparency-first approach — optional video proof of preparation
  • Subscription-based delivery — recurring orders for households, gyms, pets, and businesses
  • Zero-waste model — pet meat utilizes portions typically discarded
  • Scalable backend architecture — microservices with API gateway
  • AI-assisted ordering & tracking — smart recommendations and inventory forecasting
